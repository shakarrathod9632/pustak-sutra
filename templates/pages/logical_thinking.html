Logical Thinking & Problem Solving

Logical thinking helps children analyze problems, find smart solutions, and make better decisions. Our program develops independent thinkers who approach challenges with confidence and creativity.

WHY IT MATTERS

Why Logical Thinking is Important for Children

Stronger Problem-Solving Ability

Children learn to break down problems, identify solutions, and work through challenges step by step.

Improved Analytical Thinking

Students learn to examine information carefully, find patterns, and draw logical conclusions.

Better Decision Making

Children learn to weigh options, consider outcomes, and make thoughtful choices in academics and life.

Increased Creativity

Logical thinking encourages children to think differently, explore multiple solutions, and generate new ideas.

COURSE CONTENT

What Students Will Learn

Pattern Recognition

Children learn to identify, create, and extend patterns – a fundamental skill for logical thinking.

Logical Puzzles

Engaging puzzles help children develop step-by-step thinking and persistence in finding solutions.

Reasoning Skills

Problem Solving Strategies

Analytical Thinking

Creative Problem Solving

AGE APPROPRIATE

Level Based Syllabus

1

LKG – UKG

Key Topics Covered:

  • Basic pattern recognition (colors, shapes, sizes)
  • Matching and sorting activities
  • Simple sequencing (what comes next)
  • Visual discrimination games

Thinking Skills Developed:

Recognize patterns, sort objects by attributes, and develop early observation skills.

2

Class 1 – Class 2

Key Topics Covered:

  • Simple logic puzzles
  • Odd one out activities
  • Basic reasoning exercises
  • Following multi-step instructions

Thinking Skills Developed:

Solve simple puzzles, think logically, and follow step-by-step processes.

3

Class 3 – Class 5

Key Topics Covered:

  • Advanced logic puzzles
  • Reasoning and analogy exercises
  • Strategy games (board games, riddles)
  • Problem solving challenges

Thinking Skills Developed:

Apply logic to complex problems, think strategically, and develop reasoning skills.

4

Class 6 – Class 8

Key Topics Covered:

  • Critical thinking exercises
  • Complex problem solving scenarios
  • Brain teasers and mind puzzles
  • Decision making challenges

Thinking Skills Developed:

Think critically, analyze complex situations, and make thoughtful decisions independently.
