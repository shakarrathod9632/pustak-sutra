AGE APPROPRIATE

Level Based Syllabus

1

LKG – UKG

Key Learning Topics:

  • Alphabet recognition and phonics
  • Basic vocabulary through pictures
  • Simple words and sentences
  • Listening and speaking through stories

Skill Development Goals:

Recognize letters, understand basic words, and develop listening skills.

2

Class 1 – Class 2

Key Learning Topics:

  • Sentence formation
  • Basic grammar (nouns, verbs, adjectives)
  • Reading short stories
  • Simple writing exercises

Skill Development Goals:

Form simple sentences, understand basic grammar, and read with understanding.

3

Class 3 – Class 5

Key Learning Topics:

  • Advanced grammar (tenses, prepositions)
  • Paragraph writing
  • Vocabulary building
  • Comprehension passages

Skill Development Goals:

Write paragraphs, use correct grammar, and understand longer texts.

4

Class 6 – Class 8

Key Learning Topics:

  • Essay and letter writing
  • Advanced vocabulary
  • Critical reading
  • Creative writing

Skill Development Goals:

Express ideas fluently in writing, read critically, and build strong communication skills.
