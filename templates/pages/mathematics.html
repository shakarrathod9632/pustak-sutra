COURSE CONTENT

What Students Will Learn

Number Sense & Counting

Understanding Patterns

AGE APPROPRIATE

Level Based Syllabus

1

LKG – UKG

Key Topics Covered:

  • Number recognition (1 to 50)
  • Counting objects and sequencing
  • Basic shapes (circle, square, triangle)
  • Comparison (big/small, more/less)

Skills Students Will Develop:

Recognize numbers, count confidently, and understand basic shapes and comparisons.

2

Class 1 – Class 2

Key Topics Covered:

  • Addition and subtraction (up to 2 digits)
  • Place value (ones and tens)
  • Simple word problems
  • Introduction to multiplication tables

Skills Students Will Develop:

Perform basic operations, understand place value, and solve simple word problems.

3

Class 3 – Class 5

Key Topics Covered:

  • Multiplication and division
  • Fractions and decimals basics
  • Geometry (perimeter, area, angles)
  • Multi-step word problems

Skills Students Will Develop:

Master multiplication and division, understand fractions, and solve complex problems.

4

Class 6 – Class 8

Key Topics Covered:

  • Advanced fractions and decimals
  • Ratio, proportion, and percentages
  • Algebraic expressions
  • Data handling and graphs

Skills Students Will Develop:

Apply math to real-world situations, think algebraically, and analyze data confidently.
